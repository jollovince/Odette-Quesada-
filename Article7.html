OPM Icons Odette Quesada and Ogie Alcasid Bring “Love, Q&A” to California

Published May 28, 2025

CALIFORNIA, USA — Two of Original Pilipino Music’s (OPM) most beloved icons are set to captivate audiences in the heart of California’s wine country. On Friday, June 6, 2025, at 8:00 p.m., the Chumash Casino Resort in Santa Ynez, California will host the U.S. kickoff of “Love, Q&A”, a concert series featuring award-winning songwriter Odette Quesada and multi-hyphenate performer Ogie Alcasid.

The event marks a rare onstage reunion for the two Filipino music legends, whose careers have shaped the sound of OPM for more than four decades. “Love, Q&A” is more than just a concert — it’s a celebration of timeless hits that continue to bring generations together through music.

A Night of Classics and Connection

Known for penning some of the most enduring anthems of love and longing — “Till I Met You,” “Friend of Mine,” “To Love Again” — Odette Quesada has built a remarkable legacy as one of the Philippines’ most respected songwriters. Her music defined the sound of young love in the 1980s and remains deeply cherished today.

Joining her is Ogie Alcasid, the powerhouse balladeer and television personality behind OPM classics like “Nandito Ako,” “Sa Kanya,” and “Kailangan Ko’y Ikaw.” With his soulful voice, humor, and charm, Ogie adds his own energy and wit to every performance, complementing Odette’s heartfelt storytelling.

Together, the duo promises an unforgettable night filled with melodic storytelling, heartwarming banter, and nostalgia. “This show is about bringing back the feelings that made these songs unforgettable,” Odette said in a recent interview. “It’s about reminding ourselves how music connects people.”

“Love, Q&A” is produced by TFC Events and AIP Entertainment, following the success of Odette’s sold-out anniversary concerts in Manila last year. The U.S. leg will serve as the first stop in a series of shows planned across North America.
