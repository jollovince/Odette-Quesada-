IM PERFECT

IM PERFECT is a beautiful declaration, an album of grace and introspection released in 2015, showcasing the artistry of a master storyteller. Odette Quesada steps back to the microphone to share compositions originating from stillness and life experience, delivering new classics that feel instantly familiar and demonstrating that her songwriting remains profoundly personal. The compositions are intimate and often stripped down, yet rich with the raw emotion that has consistently characterized her work.

This collection is a gift of vulnerability, offering tracks that resonate with the struggles and triumphs of modern life. Notable inclusions are the deeply personal and therapeutic piece I Start to Cry, which was written to process the grief following the passing of her grandmother, and the charming, whimsical track Starlight, which originated from a playful challenge with Bodjie Dasig to compose a song that sounded like it came out of a Disney movie.
